Movie Star Murder

Difficulty:☆☆☆

Required Team Size: 2-6

Story:

There has been a murder at Shookespeare Productions on the set of their new upcoming film, 'Hell to Pay'. The Lincolnshire Police Department has been on the case, but now, they need you and your team of training detectives to help them catch the killer.

4 suspects, a lawsuit and a 60 minuete deadline.

Can you catch the killer?

Disclaimer

This performance will involve live actors that you will need to interact with to complete the murder mystery.

Actors will not be permitted to make any physical contact with audience members.

Audience members are not permitted to physically touch the actors, with exceptions to required adjustments both disscused and agreed upon before the event (i.e, communication adjustments.).
